An analogy: The app we are creating will exist on only 1 device (and we don't need it to work on multiple devices). We can imagine it as being installed on a single self-service kiosk in a ticket booking office. In the beginning, there are no events and no accounts on the system. First, entertainment providers come to the kiosk, register for accounts, and manually enter details to create new events. Consumers can then come in, register at the kiosk, and make bookings. To cancel an event, the entertainment providers have to come in again, login to the kiosk and use it to cancel their event.
